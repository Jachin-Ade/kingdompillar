Overview

Kingdom Pillars Digital Media Technology is a leading digital media and technology company based in Ibadan, Nigeria. Founded by Dr. Adetola Adesegun Samson, a renowned scholar and media professional, the company offers a wide range of services in photography, digital literacy, digital marketing, radio and television productions, public relations, jingles and advertisement, web designing, and other information technology-related designs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative and high-quality services that meet the evolving needs of our clients, while leveraging our expertise in media studies and academic research to drive growth and development in our community.

Our Vison

Our vision is to become a leading digital media and technology company in Nigeria, recognized for our expertise, creativity, and commitment to excellence.

Our Services

Photography: We offer professional photography services for events, portraits, and commercial purposes, capturing the essence of your brand or special moments.

Digital Literacy: Our digital literacy programs empower individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in today's digital landscape.

Digital Marketing: We provide comprehensive digital marketing solutions, including social media management, search engine optimization, and online advertising, to help businesses reach their target audience.

Radio and Television Productions: Our team produces high-quality radio and television programs, including documentaries, commercials, and entertainment shows, that engage and inform our audience.

Public Relations: We offer expert public relations services, including crisis management, media relations, and reputation management, to help organizations build and maintain a positive image.

Jingles and Advertisement: Our creative team produces catchy jingles and advertisements that capture the attention of your target audience and leave a lasting impression.

Web Designing: We design and develop professional websites that showcase your brand's unique identity and provide a seamless user experience.

Information Technology-related Designs: Our team provides innovative solutions in graphic design, video production, and animation, helping businesses stand out in a competitive market.

Extension Activities: We engage in extension activities that promote digital literacy, media education, and community development, fostering a culture of innovation and progress.

Media Studies and Academic Research: Our team conducts research and analysis in media studies, providing insights and recommendations that inform policy and practice in the media industry.

Our Team

Our team is led by Dr. Adetola Adesegun Samson, a distinguished scholar and media professional with a wealth of experience in Mass Communication, Development Communication, and Agricultural Extension. Our team members are highly skilled and dedicated professionals who share a passion for innovation, creativity, and excellence.
